ሰላም (Selam) – “Peace” – general greeting, like “Hello.”

እንደምን አለህ? (Endemin alleh?) – “How are you?” (to a male)

እንደምን አለሽ? (Endemin allesh?) – “How are you?” (to a female)

እንደምን አላችሁ? (Endemin allachu?) – “How are you all?” (plural)

ጤና ይስጥልኝ (Tena yistilign) – Literally “Health be upon you,” formal and respectful greeting.

Time-specific greetings:

ማለዳ ሰላም (Maledda selam) – Good morning

ከሰዓት በኋላ ሰላም (Kes’at behuala selam) – Good afternoon

ማታ ሰላም (Mata selam) – Good evening
